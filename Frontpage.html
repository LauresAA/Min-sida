Facts about Cats


Food

Cats are meat eaters, plain and simple.
They have to have protein from meat for a strong heart, good vision, and a healthy reproductive system.
They also like fish which have a lot of omega-3 fatty acids, which help your cat's eyes stay sharp as well as help with arthritis,
kidney disease, and heart disorders.
Eggs are another super source of protein for your cat.

Hobbies

Cats love to observe the world as it passes by. They love to perch on a windowsill and just watch. They like to stalk,
Cats will stalk toys, string, laser lights, anything that you use to play with them. Plus, it gives them the daily workout they need to keep themselves looking so good.

Lifespan

The average lifespan of domestic cats has increased in recent decades. It has risen from seven years in the 1980s, to nine years in 1995, to about 15 years in 2021
They also don't have 9 lives, unfortunately.
